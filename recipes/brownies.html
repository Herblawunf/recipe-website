Brownies

The best fudgy brownies you will ever eat, these delicious treats are guaranteed to make your mouth water, and your plate clean!

Ingredients

  • 8 oz good-quality chocolate(225 g), semi-sweet
  • 12 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1 ¼ cups sugar(250 g)
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • ¾ cup all-purpose flour(95 g)
  • ¼ cup cocoa powder(30 g)
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
